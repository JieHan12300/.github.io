About Me

Postdoc, Loughborough University

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the School of Aeronautical and Automotive Engineering, Loughborough University, working under the guidance of Prof. Jun Yang. I earned my Ph.D. from Chongqing University, where I conducted research in the New Energy Vehicle Lab under the supervision of Prof. Xiaosong Hu. My research interests include energy management, advanced electric drives, and connected and autonomous vehicles.
